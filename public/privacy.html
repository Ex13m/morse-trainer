Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

Overview

Morse Trainer ("the App") is a free educational application for learning Morse code. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

  • Settings (tone frequency, dash threshold, speed, vibration, language, theme) — stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • Statistics (best WPM, last WPM, phrases completed) — stored locally in your browser's localStorage.
  • Microphone access — used only for real-time Morse code decoding when you explicitly activate the microphone feature. Audio is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded or transmitted.
  • Speech Recognition — used only for voice-to-text input when you explicitly activate the voice feature. Speech data is processed by your browser's built-in speech recognition service (e.g., Google Speech Services on Chrome). We do not control or store this data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts — the App loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google's privacy policy applies to this service.
  • Browser Speech API — if you use voice input, your browser may send audio to its cloud speech recognition service. Refer to your browser's privacy policy for details.

Cookies

The App does not use cookies. It uses localStorage for storing your preferences and progress on your device only.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

To delete all stored data, clear your browser's site data for this application, or use the "Reset to Defaults" button in the App's Settings tab.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
